Trusted contacts help Eternum verify your status and release recovery shares after death verification. They do not receive vault plaintext.

## Responsibilities

Trusted contacts can:

- Receive check-in escalation emails
- Confirm death or contest a false alarm
- Upload evidence during verification
- Activate and later release their assigned recovery share

They cannot:

- Read your vault items
- Recover your vault alone
- Release shares before death verification allows it

## Activation flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="You add a trusted contact">
    Eternum sends an email telling them they were added.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You assign a recovery share">
    Share assignment creates a portal link for that trusted contact.
  </Step>
  <Step title="They enter the bootstrap key">
    The bootstrap key is shared out-of-band. It unlocks the initial encrypted share.
  </Step>
  <Step title="They set a passphrase">
    The contact protects their private key locally with a passphrase.
  </Step>
  <Step title="They become active">
    Once activated, their share can be released later if verification completes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Contact limits by plan

| Plan | Trusted contacts |
| --- | --- |
| Seed | 2 |
| Legacy | 5 |
| Estate | Unlimited |

<Info>
Use contacts who are likely to respond and who do not share the same email account or device access.
</Info>
